Concert Rules and Regulations

1. Please arrive at least 30 minutes before the concert starts.

2. No outside food or drinks are allowed inside the venue.

3. Keep your mobile phones on silent mode during the performance.

4. Respect the performers and fellow audience members.

5. Photography and recording are not allowed without permission.

6. Follow any additional rules announced by the event staff.
